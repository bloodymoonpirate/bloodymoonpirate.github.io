## 1. Equicorrelation

If all $n$ assets share the same pair-wise correlation $\rho$, then:

```math
R(\rho) \;=\;
\begin{pmatrix}
1      & \rho & \dots & \rho \\
\rho   & 1    & \dots & \rho \\
\vdots &      & \ddots& \vdots\\
\rho   & \rho & \dots & 1
\end{pmatrix}.
